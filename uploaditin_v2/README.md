# Upload It In — User Interface

Welcome to the frontend application for **Upload It In**. This interface provides a clean, responsive, and intuitive experience for all our users.

---

## 👩‍🏫 For Teachers

Managing your classes has never been easier. As a teacher, you can:
- **Create and Manage Classes:** Easily set up new classes and generate invite codes to share with your students.
- **Post Assignments:** Upload your model answers to create new assignments quickly.
- **Track Progress:** View real-time submission status and instantly see automatically generated scores for all your students in one dashboard.

## 🎓 For Students

We designed this portal to make your academic life smoother. As a student, you can:
- **Enroll in Seconds:** Join your teacher's class instantly using their provided invite code.
- **Simple Submissions:** Upload your essay answers easily using PDF, DOCX, or TXT formats.
- **Instant Feedback:** View your grades as soon as your assignment is evaluated by our smart system.

---

## ⚙️ For Administrators

Keep the platform running smoothly with our comprehensive admin tools:
- **User Management:** Oversee all teacher and student accounts to ensure a safe learning environment.
- **Platform Analytics:** Get a bird's-eye view of total classes, active assignments, and system usage.
- **Content Control:** Easily customize and update the platform's landing page content.
